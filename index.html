⛳ Competition Rules - The Masters 2026

  • Pick 1 golfer from each of the 8 brackets (brackets based on odds for top 64 players) to form your team of 8.
  • Your team score is calculated from the best 4 scores out of 8 (relative to par).
  • The lowest combined score wins.
  • If a golfer withdraws before the start, you get the next golfer below in the bracket (or the top golfer if at the bottom). You can also swap to another golfer in that bracket before the deadline.
  • If a golfer withdraws or is disqualified during the tournament, a score of +20 is recorded.
  • If a golfer misses the cut, he gets +5 for each missed round (normally +10 total) added to his 2-round score.
  • An extra -3 bonus is applied to the golfer who wins the tournament.
  • If 2 competitors are tied, all 8 golfers on their team count to break the tie.
  • Only one entry per person — entrants must be over 18 as at 01 Jan 2026.
  • Picks must be submitted before 10am on Thursday 9th April.
