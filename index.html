⛳ Competition Rules - The Masters 2026

  • Each player picks 10 golfers from the tournament field.
  • Your team score is calculated from the best 5 out of 10 golfers' scores relative to par.
  • The lowest combined score wins.
  • Golfers who miss the cut receive a penalty score (cut line + penalty strokes).
  • Golfers who withdraw (WD) or are disqualified (DQ) receive a +20 penalty.
  • If a golfer is not in the field / doesn't start, they receive a +10 default score.
  • Picks must be submitted before the first tee time on Thursday.
  • Golfers can be picked by multiple teams (no draft).
